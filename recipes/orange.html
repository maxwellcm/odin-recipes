Orange Julius

Description

A scruptious treat for a hot summer afternoon, an orange julius will keep you feel fresh and fly.

Ingredients

  • 1 cupe Frozen orange juice concentrate

Steps

  1. Put all Ingredients in a blender.
  2. Pour and serve :)
