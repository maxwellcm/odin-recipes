Pigs in a Blanket

Description

The only thing better than snuggling with a chonky pet is making bite sized chonkers you can eat! This is an amazing appetizer that gives you the warm and fuzzy homebody feels. Simply enought it only takes some store bought dough and little wieners you can pick up at any grocery store.

Ingredients

  • 1 (8-oz.) tube crescent rolls
  • 1 12-oz. package mini cocktail weiners
  • 4 tbsp. melted butter
  • Coarse salt, for sprinkling

Steps

  1. Prehead oven to 375. On a floured surface, unroll crescent sheets and tear where perforated. Cut each triangle into 3 smaller triangles.
  2. Place one cocktail weiner on thick side of each triangle then gently roll to thinner side.
  3. Transfer to a medium baking sheet, brush with melted butter, and a sprinkle with coarse salt.
  4. Bake until golden brown, ~13 minutes.
