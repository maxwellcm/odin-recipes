Milkshake

Description

Smooth, cool, and nostalgic are all things I find to be exemplified by a milkshake. Easy to make any flavor you want (we are sticking with chocolate) just add your favorite treats.

Ingredients

  • Ice Cream
  • Milk
  • Chocolate syrup

Steps

  1. Add milk and ice cream into the blender.
  2. Add choclate syrup to desired preference
  3. Optional: add anything else you would like! Forget the chocolate and add Strawberries? You got it!
